Digital rights management (DRM) restricts the playback or duplication of music to enforce copyright. Music files may only be played on specific devices or restricted by the number of playbacks. <Vg k="RTEE_DRM" /> enables you to find and play DRM music.

## Understand the tech

![play-drm-music](\images\video-sdk\play-drm-music.svg)

## Prerequisites

To use the <Vg k="RTEE_DRM" /> extension, you must meet the following requirements:

- Implemented <Link to="../get-started/get-started-sdk">Get Started with <Vpd k="NAME"/></Link>.
<PlatformWrapper platform="ios">
- Minimum device requirements
    - iOS iPhone 6S
    - iPadOS
    - iPad Pro (2nd generation)
    - iPad mini (5th generation)
    - iPad Air (3rd generation)
    - macOS MacBook Pro 2015
- Ensure that the dynamical extension library `AgoraDrmLoader.framework` is integrated into your project.
