This guide explains how to configure and apply various watermarks when recording video with the <Vg k="OPREC_SDK" />.

## Understand the tech

You can add watermarks to your recordings to display company logos, timestamps, or text for branding, copyright, or verification purposes. The <Vg k="OPREC_SDK" /> supports three types of watermarks in both individual recording and composite recording modes:

- **Text watermark**: Display custom text with configurable font and size.
- **Timestamp watermark**: Display the current time from the recording server.
- **Image watermark**: Display a static image (PNG or JPG).

## Prerequisites

Before proceeding, integrate the <Vg k="OPREC_SDK" /> and complete the [Quickstart](/on-premise-recording/get-started) guide to ensure basic recording functionality is in place.
