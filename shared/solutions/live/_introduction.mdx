
Agora Live Solution enable viewers and hosts to interact and share more easily and enjoyably. Agora's live solution supports the following scenarios:

* Single-Host Live Streaming: Viewers can join the live room to watch the live stream, send messages, and give gifts to the host.
* Hosts PK : To enhance the live streaming atmosphere and quickly attract more followers, a host can invite the host of another live room to engage in audio or video connection for interactive communication or online PK. Viewers in both live rooms can watch the interaction between the two hosts simultaneously and give gifts, vote, etc., based on their performance.
* Viewer-Host Audio Connection: Viewers can engage in real-time audio and video interactions with the host, allowing other users in the room to also watch the live stream.
