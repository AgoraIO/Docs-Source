

### Submit a Ticket

This page introduces how to submit a ticket in Agora Console.

Follow these steps:

1.  Log in to [Agora Console](https://console.agora.io/).

2.  Click **Support** in the top navigation menu.

    ![1638341776363](https://web-cdn.agora.io/docs-files/1638341776363)

3.  Type in your question or keywords to see if the question has been answered. If you cannot find your answer, select a category and submit a ticket to Agoraâ€™s customer support.

You can track the status of your ticket under **Recent Tickets**.
