In multi-host streaming scenarios where 4 or more hosts stream videos simultaneously, users with the audience role face the following issues:

- Device performance and downstream bandwidth pressure: Subscribing to multiple video streams requires high device performance and bandwidth, especially on mid-to-low-end devices. In such scenarios, audience users may experience lag and frame dropping, leading to a negative experience.

- Flexible layout requirement: There is a need for custom video layout, that enables viewers to adjust the position of each host's video to meet individual viewing preference.

- Window switching and zooming need: With multiple hosts, the size of each host's video is limited. Flexible switching, and window size adjustment enables a "thousand people, thousand faces" viewing experience.

<Vg k="COMPANY" /> provides a video experience optimization scheme for multi-host video scenarios, implemented through **client-customized composite layout**, centered on the viewer, to create a smooth and personalized video viewing experience.

This solution can be widely applied to the following scenarios:

- Team battle PK
- Multi-person conference
- Online education for large classes
