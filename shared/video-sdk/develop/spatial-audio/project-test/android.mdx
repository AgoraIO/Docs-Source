7. **Test spatial audio effects for remote users**

    1. Put on earphones connected to the <Vpl k="NAME"/> test device.

    1. Speak into the microphone connected to the web demo app. Note the quality of audio playing through your earphones.

    1. Move the slider to the right. Your <Vpl k="CLIENT" /> updates the position of the remote user in the spatial audio engine by increasing the distance. 
    
    1. Speak into the microphone again and listen to the audio through your earphones. You feel that the remote user has moved away from you.

8. **Test spatial audio effects for media player**

    1. To setup spatial audio position of your media player, add [Media Playing](/video-calling/develop/play-media) to your <Vpl k="CLIENT" />. 
    
    1. Replace the call to `localSpatial.updateRemotePosition` in `updateSpatialAudioPosition` with the following:

        ```java
        // Update spatial position of the media player
        localSpatial.updatePlayerPositionInfo(mediaPlayer.getMediaPlayerId(), positionInfo);
        ```
    
    1. Play the media file in your <Vpl k="NAME"/> <Vpl k="CLIENT" />.

    1. Connect your earphones to the web demo and note the quality of audio playing.

    1. Move the slider to the right. Your <Vpl k="CLIENT" /> updates the position of the media player in the spatial audio engine. 

        Note the change in quality of media playing through your earphones. You feel that the position of the media player has moved away from you.
