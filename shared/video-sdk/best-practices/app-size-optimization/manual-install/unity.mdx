1. Unzip <Vpd k="SDK" /> from [the Unity download](../../sdks) to a local folder.

1. In in your <Vpl k="NAME" /> Editor, click **Assets > Import Package > Custom Package**.

1. Navigate to the <Vpd k="SDK" /> package and click *Open*.

1. In **Import Unity Package**, click **Import**.

<Vpd k="SDK" /> objects are included in your game.
