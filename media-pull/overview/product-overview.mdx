Agora <Vg k="MPULL" /> enables the injection of an external media stream into a real-time Agora channel, allowing the hosts and audience to see and hear the additional stream while interacting with each other. The <Vg k="MPULL" /> extension works with <Vg k="AUDIO" /> , <Vg k="VIDEO" />, and <Vg k="ILS" />.
