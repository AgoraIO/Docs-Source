This page describes how <Vg k="COMPANY"/> calculates and bills for <Vpd k="NAME"/> usage.

When you use <Vpd k="NAME" /> in your project, <Vg k="COMPANY"/> charges a monthly fee based on your usage across all projects under your developer account. At the end of each month, the free quota is subtracted from your total usage, and the remaining minutes are multiplied by the unit price to calculate your bill, rounded to two decimal places. For general billing information, see [Billing](/realtime-communication/reference/billing-policies#billing).

<Admonition type="info">
If you have signed a sales contract with Agora, your billing follows the terms in your contract.
</Admonition>

## Unit price

Creating a <Vpd k="NAME" /> instance using the RESTful API and joining a channel incurs an audio task fee at the following rate:

  <thead>
    <tr>

      <th style={{ textAlign: 'right' }}>Pricing (USD / minute)</th>
      <th>Free Minutes</th>
    </tr>
  </thead>
  <tbody>
    <tr>
      <td>Conversational AI Engine Audio Task</td>
      <td style={{ textAlign: 'right' }}>0.10*</td>
      <td> First 300 minutes are free</td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <td colSpan="3" style={{ fontSize: '0.9rem', fontStyle: 'italic', paddingTop: '0.25rem' }}>*The unit price includes usage of selected ASR, LLM, and TTS models. You will be charged the same price even if you bring your own key (BYOK).</td>
    </tr>
  </tbody>
</table>

Usage of ASR, LLM, and TTS providers is included in the unit price when using an Agora managed key. Agora provides and manages the API keys for the following providers:

- **ASR**
  - ARES
  - Deepgram nova‑2
  - Deepgram nova‑3
- **LLM**
  - OpenAI GPT‑4o‑mini
  - OpenAI GPT‑4.1‑mini
  - OpenAI GPT‑5‑nano
  - OpenAI GPT‑5‑mini
- **TTS**
  - MiniMax 2.6 Turbo
  - MiniMax 2.8 Turbo
  - OpenAI TTS‑1
