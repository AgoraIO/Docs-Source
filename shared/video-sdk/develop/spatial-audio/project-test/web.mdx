5. To connect to a channel, click **Join**.

6. Connect your binaural audio playback device to your development machine. Speak into the microphone connected to the web demo app. You hear the audio through your audio playback device.

7. Use the `+` and `-` buttons to change the distance. Speak into the microphone connected to the web demo app. Note the change in the quality of audio playing through the playback device. When you increase the distance, you feel that the remote user has moved away from you. When you decrease the distance you feel that the remote user is closer to you.

8. Press the **Play audio file** button.

    You hear the audio file being played through your playback device. 

9. Use the `+` and `-` buttons to change the distance. When you increase the distance, you feel that the media player has moved away from you. When you decrease the distance you feel that the media player is closer to you.
