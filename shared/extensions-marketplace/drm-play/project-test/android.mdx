<PlatformWrapper platform="android">

1. [Generate a temporary token](../reference/manage-agora-account#generate-a-temporary-token) in <Vg k="CONSOLE" /> .

1. In your browser, navigate to the <Link target="_blank" to="https://webdemo.agora.io/dualStream/index.html"><Vg k="COMPANY" /> dual stream web demo</Link> and update _App ID_, _Channel_, and _Token_ with the values for your temporary token, then click **Join**.

1. In Android Studio, open `app/java/com.example.<projectname>/MainActivity`, and update `appId`, `channelName` and `token` with the values for your temporary token.

1. Generate an [rtm token](https://webdemo.agora.io/token-builder/) and update the `rtmToken` variable with the value.

1. Connect a physical Android device to your development device.

1. In Android Studio, click *Run app*. A moment later you see the project installed on your device.

    If this is the first time you run the project, grant microphone and camera access to your <Vpl k="CLIENT" />.

1. Press **Search Music**. 
    
    You see a message notifying you of the search results.

1. Press **Play Music**.

    Your selected song is played.
