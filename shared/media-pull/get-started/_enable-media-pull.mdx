To enable <Vg k="MPULL" /> in <Vg k="CONSOLE" />:

1. In [Agora Console](https://console.agora.io/), open **Project Management**.

1. On the **Project Management** page, click **Config** for the project you want to enable <Vg k="MPULL" /> in.

1. Find **<Vg k="MPULL" />**, and click **Enable**.

1. Click **Enable <Vg k="MPULL" />**

1. In **Media <Vg k="MPULL" /> - Duration - Settings**, click **Apply**.

1. Click **Apply**.

You can now use <Vg k="MPULL" />. You see the usage statistics in the **Usage** page.
