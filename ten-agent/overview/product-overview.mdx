TEN Agent is a conversational AI agent powered by the TEN framework. It integrates LLMs, audio and video Gemini 2.0 Live, OpenAI Realtime, RTC, and more. It delivers real-time capabilities to see, hear, and speak, while being fully compatible with popular workflow platforms like Dify and Coze.
