The <Vg k="AI_FRAMEWORK" /> enables you to rapidly build and deploy seamless AI experiences across voice, video, and chat, with ultra-low latency and top-tier network performance. You can quickly connect framework extensions, which come with pre-integrated connections to leading Large Language Models and cloud services, even if they are developed in different programming languages. Use the graph designer to visually interconnect extensions, establishing relationships and data flow between them. With the <Vg k="AI_FRAMEWORK" /> package manager, you can download or share your extensions on the extension store.
