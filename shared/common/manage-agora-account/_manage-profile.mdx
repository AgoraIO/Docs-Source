

## Manage Profile

This page shows how to view and edit the profile of your Agora account.

### Enter the Profile page

To enter the *Profile* page, follow these steps:

1. Log in to [Agora Console](https://console.agora.io/).
2. Click your account name in the top-right corner, and select your account name from the dropdown menu.

![Image](https://web-cdn.agora.io/docs-files/1638267106951)

On the *Profile* page, you can view and edit your language setting, phone number, email address, password, and login methods.

### Change language setting

1. Enter the [Profile page](https://sso2.agora.io/en/profile).
2. In the dropdown menu of *Language*, select *English* or *中文*.
3. Click *Save*.

### Change phone number, email address, or password

1.Enter the [Profile page](https://sso2.agora.io/en/profile).
2.Find *Phone*, *E-mail*, or *Password*, then click *Update* or *Reset*.
3.Follow the on-screen instructions to complete.

### Reset your password

If you forget your password, follow these steps to reset it:

1. Go to the [login page](https://sso.agora.io/login/), and click *Forget password*.
2. Fill in your email address, and click *Send email*. Agora sends an email to you.
3. Follow the instructions in the email to reset your password.

### Link a third-party account

Follow these steps to link a third-party account with your Agora account:

1. Enter the [Profile page](https://sso2.agora.io/en/profile).
2. Find *GitHub*, *WeChat*, or *Google*, then click *Connect*.
3. Follow the on-screen instructions to complete.
