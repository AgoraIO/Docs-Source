1. Open the [<Vg k="GET_STARTED"/>](../get-started/get-started-sdk) <Vpd k="NAME"/> project you created previously.

2. Add the Spatial Audio Extension to your <Vpl k="CLIENT" />:

    1. In the package manager, Ensure that you have included the **AgoraSpatialAudioExtension** binary from the **AgoraRtcKit** package.

    2. In the project navigator, open **Build Phases** in the target for your project. Then, add the *SpatialAudio** binary in **Link Binary With Libraries**.
