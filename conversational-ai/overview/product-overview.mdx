<Vg k="COMPANY" />'s <Vpd k="NAME" /> is a cloud-based orchestration that enables real-time voice, video, and avatar interactions between users and AI agents. Build natural conversational experiences with ultra-low latency, bring your own AI models from 15+ providers. Deploy across web, mobile, and telephony with a unified architecture. The engine handles real-time audio transport, session management, and AI pipeline orchestration at scale, so you can focus on creating intelligent conversational experiences.
