To enable <Vg k="MPULL" /> in <Vg k="CONSOLE" />:

1. Log in to <Link to="{{Global.AGORA_CONSOLE_URL}}"><Vg k="CONSOLE" /></Link>, and click **Projects** in the left navigation panel.

2. On the **Project Management** page, find the project for which you want to enable the <Vg k="MPULL" /> service, and click **Config**.

    <img src="https://web-cdn.agora.io/docs-files/1641971834422"/>

3. Under **Real-time engagement extensions**, find **<Vg k="MPULL" />**, and click **Enable**.


4. Click **Enable <Vg k="MPULL" />**.

5. Click **Apply**.

You can now use <Vg k="MPULL" />. You see the usage statistics in the **Usage** page.
