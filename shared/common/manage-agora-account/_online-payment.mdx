
## Billing Center

This page shows how to check your account balance, add money to your account, view transactions, and make a withdrawal in Agora Console.

### Restrictions

If your account has multiple members, only those with the role of *Admin*, *Finance*, or an authorized custom role have access to the *Billing Center* page.

###Check account balance

After logging in to [Agora Console](https://console.agora.io/), you can see your account balance in the *Billing Center* panel on the *Overview* page.

### Add money to account

You can add money to your account either with a credit card or via bank transfer.

#### Credit card

To add money to your account with a credit card, follow these steps:

1.In [Billing Center](https://console.agora.io/finance), click *Credit Card* in the left navigation panel.
2. (Optional) If you have not added a credit card to your account, follow these steps:
    1. Click *Add a card*.
    2. Enter the card number, month, year, and CVC.
    3. Choose whether or not to set this card as the default credit card.
    4. Click *Save*.
3.Select a credit card, enter the *Payment Amount*, and click *Pay*. If the payment is successful, you see a "Payment Succeeded"message.

#### Bank transfer

To add money to your account via bank transfer, follow these steps:

1. In [Billing Center](https://console.agora.io/finance), click *Bank Transfer* in the left navigation panel.
2. Follow the on-screen instructions to complete.

### View transactions

In [Billing Center](https://console.agora.io/finance), click *Transactions* in the left navigation panel. You can see all the transactions in your account.

You can also click *Export CSV* in the top-right corner to export the record of transactions as a CSV file.

### Make a withdrawal

You can withdraw from your Agora account balance for free. Follow these steps to make a withdrawal:

1. In [Billing Center](https://console.agora.io/finance), click *Withdraw* in the left navigation menu.
2. Click *Request Withdrawal* in the top-right corner.
3. Specify the amount you want to withdraw, and click *OK*.

After submitting a withdrawal request, you can track the request status on the *Withdraw* page.

Agora processes your withdrawal request within 10 business days. However, if your actual usage in the current month exceeds 10,000 minutes, your request is processed after the next billing day. Ensure that you have sufficient funds in your account to cover any pending charges.

After your request is approved, your money is returned to your credit card.
