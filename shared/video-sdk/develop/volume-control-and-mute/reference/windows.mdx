### Sample Projects
<Vg k="COMPANY" /> offers an open-source example project for adjusting collection, playback, and in-ear monitoring volumes for your reference. You can go there to download or view the source code.

        * Gitee: [AudioVoume](https://gitee.com/agoraio-community/API-Examples/tree/main/windows/APIExample/APIExample/Advanced/AudioVolume)
        * GitHub: [AudioVoume](https://github.com/AgoraIO/API-Examples/tree/main/windows/APIExample/APIExample/Advanced/AudioVolume)

If you need to adjust the playback volume of mixing or audio effect files, you can refer to the following sample projects:

        * Gitee: [AudioMixing](https://gitee.com/agoraio-community/API-Examples/tree/main/windows/APIExample/APIExample/Advanced/AudioMixing) or [AudioEffect](https://gitee.com/agoraio-community/API-Examples/tree/main/windows/APIExample/APIExample/Advanced/AudioEffect)
        * GitHub: [AudioMixing](https://github.com/AgoraIO/API-Examples/tree/main/windows/APIExample/APIExample/Advanced/AudioMixing) or [AudioEffect](https://github.com/AgoraIO/API-Examples/tree/main/windows/APIExample/APIExample/Advanced/AudioEffect)
