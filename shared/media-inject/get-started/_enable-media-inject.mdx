Follow these steps to enable the Media Inject service in Agora Console:

1. Log in to [Agora Console](https://console.agora.io/), and click the **Project Management** icon on the left navigation panel.

2. On the **Project Management** page, find the project for which you want to enable the Media Inject service, and click **Config**.

    <img src="https://web-cdn.agora.io/docs-files/1641971834422"/>

3. Under **Real-time engagement extensions**, find **Media Inject**, and click **Enable**.

    <img src="https://web-cdn.agora.io/docs-files/1648535723896" />

4. Click **Enable Media Inject**.
![](https://web-cdn.agora.io/docs-files/1648541537528)
5. Click **Apply**.

Now, you can use Media Inject service and see the usage statistics in the **Usage** page.
