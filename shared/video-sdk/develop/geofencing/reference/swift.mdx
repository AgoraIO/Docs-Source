### Geofencing area codes

The supported area codes are members of
<PlatformWrapper platform="ios"><Link to="{{Global.API_REF_IOS_ROOT_RTC_KIT}}/agoraareacodetype">AgoraAreaCodeType:</Link></PlatformWrapper>
<PlatformWrapper platform="macos"><Link to="{{Global.API_REF_MACOS_ROOT_RTC_KIT}}/agoraareacodetype">AgoraAreaCodeType:</Link></PlatformWrapper>

* `AgoraAreaCodeType.global`: Global (Default)
* `AgoraAreaCodeType.CN`: Mainland China
* `AgoraAreaCodeType.NA`: North America
* `AgoraAreaCodeType.EUR`: Europe
* `AgoraAreaCodeType.AS`: Asia, excluding Mainland China
* `AgoraAreaCodeType.JP`: Japan
* `AgoraAreaCodeType.IN`: India

The area codes support bitwise operations.
