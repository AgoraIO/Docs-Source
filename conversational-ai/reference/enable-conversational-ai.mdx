## Enable Conversational AI

<Vpd k="NAME" /> is enabled by default on all new <Vg k="COMPANY" /> projects. If you are using an existing project you may need to enable it for your app ID in <Vg k="CONSOLE" />.

1. Navigate to the <Link to="{{Global.AGORA_CONSOLE_URL}}">Projects</Link> page, locate your project in the **My Projects** list and click the associated ✏️ icon to configure it.

    ![configure_project](/images/signaling/configure_project.png)

1. Under **All features** select **Conversational AI > Configurations** and toggle the switch to **Enable Conversational AI**.

    ![Console](/images/conversational-ai/enable-convo-ai-console.png)

After enabling, you can call the <Vpd k="NAME" /> RESTful API.
