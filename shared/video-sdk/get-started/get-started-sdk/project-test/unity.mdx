3. **Configure the project**:

    In `Assets/agora-manager/config.json`, Replace:

    * `appId` - with your app ID from [<Vg k="CONSOLE" />](https://console.agora.io/).
    * `channelName` - with the name of a channel you want to join.
    * `token` - with a valid temporary token for the `channelName`.

1. **Integrate the Video SDK**

    1. Unzip [the latest version of the Agora Video SDK](https://docs.agora.io/en/sdks?platform=unity) to a local 
       folder.

    1. Open the project in **Unity Editor**, then click **Assets** > **Import Package** > **Custom Package**.

    1. Navigate to the Video SDK package and click **Open**.

    1. In **Import Unity Package**, click **Import**.

1.  **Start the <Vpl k="NAME" /> reference app**:
    
    1. Click **Play** to start the game.

        A moment later you see the project running on your development device.

    1. From the main screen of the <Vpl k="CLIENT" />, choose **<Vpd k="NAME" />** from the dropdown and then select
    **SDK QuickStart**.

1. **Join a Channel**:

    <ProductWrapper product="{['interactive-live-streaming', 'broadcast-streaming']}">
    Select an option and click **Join** to start a session.
    - When you join as a **Host**, the local video is published and played in the <Vpl k="CLIENT"/>.
    - When you join as **Audience**, the remote stream is subscribed and played.
    </ProductWrapper>

    <ProductWrapper product="video-calling">
    Click **Join** to start a call. Now, you can see yourself on the device screen and talk to the remote user using your <Vpl k="CLIENT"/>.
    </ProductWrapper>
