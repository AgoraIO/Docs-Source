## Test your implementation

1. [Generate a temporary token](../get-started/manage-agora-account#generate-a-temporary-token) in <Vg k="CONSOLE" /> .

1. In your browser, navigate to the <Link target="_blank" to="{{Global.DEMO_BASIC_VIDEO_CALL_URL}}"><Vg k="COMPANY" /> web demo</Link> and update _App ID_, _Channel_, and _Token_ with the values for your temporary token, then click **Join**.

1.  In `/lib/main.dart`, update `appId`, `channelName` and `token` with the values for your temporary token.

1.  Connect a test device to your development device.

1.  In your IDE, click **Run app** or launch your app from the terminal using `flutter run lib/main.dart`. A moment later you see the project installed on your device.

    If this is the first time you run the project, grant microphone and camera access to your <Vpl k="CLIENT"/>.

1. Click **Join** to start a call. 

1. Press **Apply effect**.

    You see the cat ears effect applied to the video stream.
