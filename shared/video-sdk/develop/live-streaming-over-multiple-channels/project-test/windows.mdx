### Test channel media Relay

1. In Visual Studio, open `AgoraImplementationDlg.cpp` and update `APP_ID`, `ChannelName` and `destChannelName`.

2. [Generate a temporary token](../reference/manage-agora-account#generate-a-temporary-token) in <Vg k="CONSOLE" /> using `APP_ID` and `ChannelName`. Use it to update `Token` in `AgoraImplementationDlg.cpp`. Use the same values to generate another token and update `sourceChannelToken`.

3. Generate a third token in <Vg k="CONSOLE" /> using `APP_ID` and `destChannelName`. Use it to update `destChannelToken` in `AgoraImplementationDlg.cpp`.

4. In your browser, navigate to the <Link target="_blank" to="{{Global.DEMO_BASIC_VIDEO_CALL_URL}}"><Vg k="COMPANY" /> web demo</Link> and update _App ID_, _Channel_, and _Token_ with the values for your temporary token, then click **Join**.

5. Repeat the previous step on a second device, but this time use `APP_ID`, `destChannelName`, and `destChannelToken` to **Join** the channel.

7. In Visual Studio, click **Local Window Debugger**. A moment later, you see the project running on your development device.

    If this is the first time you run your app, grant camera and microphone permissions.

8. Select **Host** and press **Join**. You see the video from the web browser demo app connected to `ChannelName` in the local view of your development device.

9. Press **Start Channel Media Relay**. You see the video from the web browser demo app connected to `ChannelName` relayed to the web browser demo app connected to `destChannelName`.

10. Press **Stop Channel Media Relay**. The media relaying is stopped.

### Test joining multiple channels

1. In Visual Studio, open `AgoraImplementationDlg.cpp` and update `APP_ID`, `ChannelName` and `secondChannelName`.

2. [Generate a temporary token](../reference/manage-agora-account#generate-a-temporary-token) in <Vpl k="CONSOLE" /> using `APP_ID` and `ChannelName`. Use it to update `Token` in `AgoraImplementationDlg.cpp`.

3. Generate a second token in <Vpl k="CONSOLE" /> using `APP_ID` and `secondChannelName`. Use it to update `secondChannelToken` in `AgoraImplementationDlg.cpp`.

4. In your browser, navigate to the <Link target="_blank" to="{{Global.DEMO_BASIC_VIDEO_CALL_URL}}"><Vg k="COMPANY" /> web demo</Link> and update _App ID_, _Channel_, and _Token_ with the values for your temporary token, then click **Join**.

5. Repeat the previous step on a second device, but this time use `APP_ID`, `secondChannelName`, and `secondChannelToken` to **Join** the channel.

7. In Visual Studio, click **Local Window Debugger**. A moment later, you see the project running on your development device.

    If this is the first time you run your app, grant camera and microphone permissions.

8. Select **Audience** 

    1. Press **Join**. You see the video from the web browser demo app connected to `ChannelName` in the local view of your development device.
    2. Press **Join Second Channel**. You see the video from the web browser demo app connected to `secondChannelName` in the remote view of your development device.
    3. Press **Leave Second Channel** and then **Leave** to exit both channels.

9. Select **Host** 

    1. Press **Join**. You see the local video in the local view of your development device. The web browser demo app connected to `ChannelName` shows the video from your development device.
    2. Press **Join Second Channel**. You see the video from the web browser demo app connected to `secondChannelName` in the bottom frame of your development device.
    3. Press **Leave Second Channel** to exit both channels.
