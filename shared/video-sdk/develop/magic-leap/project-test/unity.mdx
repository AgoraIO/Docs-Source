3. In **Unity Editor**, in `NewBehaviourScript.cs`, update `_appID`, `_channelName` and `_token` with the values for your temporary token.

4. In **Unity Editor**, click **Magic Leap** > **ML Remote** > **Import Support Libraries**.

5. To launch Magic Leap Lab, in **Unity Editor**, click **Magic Leap** > **ML Remote** > **Launch Zero Iteration**. Magic Leap Lab launches and the Zero Iteration window appears.

6. In the **Zero Iteration** window beside **Target**, toggle **State** to **Running**.

7. In **Unity Editor**, click **Play**. A moment later you see the <Vpl k="CLIENT" /> running on your development device and the same UI is displayed in Magic Leap Lab.

8. In Magic Leap Lab, select a role using the toggle buttons.

9. In Magic Leap Lab, click **Join** to start a call. Now, you can see yourself on the Magic Leap simulator and talk to the remote user within Magic Leap environment.
