### Integrate the SDK

<Tabs groupId="language">
<TabItem value="cpp" label="C++" default>

1. Download the latest Windows [SDK](../../sdks).

1. Unzip and open the downloaded SDK. Copy all subfolders in `sdk/` to your solution folder. Make sure these subfolders are in the same directory as your `.sln` files.

#### Configure the project

In the Solution Explorer window, right-click the project name and click **Properties** to configure the following:

1. Go to the **C/C++ > General > Additional Include Directories** menu, click **Edit** , and in the pop-up window enter `$(SolutionDir)include`.
1. Go to the **Linker > General > Additional Library Directory** menu, click **Edit** , and in the pop-up window enter `$(SolutionDir)lib`.
1. Go to the **Linker > Input > Additional Dependencies** menu, click **Edit** , and in the pop-up window enter `agora_rtc_sdk.dll.lib`.

After the configuration is complete, click **OK**.

</TabItem>

<TabItem value="cs" label="C#" default>
To create a basic UI, add the following controls:

* A **PictureBox** for the local video
* A **PictureBox** for the remote video
* A **Button** to join a channel
* A **Button** to leave the channel
* A **TextBox** for the channel name

![](/images/video-sdk/video-call-ui-cs.png)

Refer to the following steps to create this UI:

1. **Create Join and Leave buttons**

    1. In your project, open the **Solution Explore** window, double-click `Form1.cs` , open the Toolbox window, and select the **Button** control. Add two buttons, and move them to suitable locations. 
    1. Move the mouse to one of the buttons, right-click, select **Properties**, and in the **Properties** window, modify the **Text** property to `Join` and the **Name** property to `btnJoin`. 
    1. Repeat the previous step to modify the properties of the second button; set the **Text** to `Leave` and the **Name** to `btnLeave`.

1. **Create local and remote view windows**

    1. Open the **Toolbox** window, select the **PictureBox** control, and add two controls for the local view and remote view. 
    1. Move the mouse to the local view control, right-click, select **Properties**, and modify the **Name** property to `videoboxLocal` in the **Properties** window; modify the **BorderStyle** to `FixedSingle`. 
    1. Repeat the previous step to modify the properties of the remote view window control; set the **Name** to `videoboxRemote` and **BorderStyle** to `FixedSingle`.

1. **Create channel name input box**

    1. Open the **Toolbox** window, and add a **TextBox** control. 
    1. Right-click **TextBox** control and modify its **Name** property to to `ChannelName`.
        
        Save your changes.

1. Double-click the **Join** and **Leave** buttons. 

    The IDE automatically associates the click event handler functions.
