<PlatformWrapper platform="unity">

1. [Generate a temporary token](../reference/manage-agora-account#generate-a-temporary-token) in <Vg k="CONSOLE" /> .

1. In your browser, navigate to the <Link target="_blank" to="{{Global.DEMO_BASIC_VIDEO_CALL_URL}}"><Vg k="COMPANY" /> web demo</Link> and update _App ID_, _Channel_, and _Token_ with the values for your temporary token, then click **Join**.

1. In **Unity Editor**, open your script file and update `_appID`, `_channelName` and `_token` with the values for your temporary token.

1. In **Unity Editor**, click **Play**. A moment later you see the <Vpl k="CLIENT" /> running on your development device.

1. Press **Join** to connect to the same channel as your web demo.

1. In `setVirtualBackground`, modify the value of `virtualBackgroundSource.source` to the absolute path of a `.jpg` or `.png` image file on your test device.

1. Press **Virtual Background**. The background is blurred in the local video of your test device and the remote video of the web demo. You see a message in the debug console confirming that background blur has been enabled.

1. Press the button again. The background changes color to a solid blue. You see a message in the debug console confirming that color background has been enabled.

1. Press the button again. You see an image in the background. You also see a message in the debug console confirming that background image has been enabled.

1. Press the button again. Virtual background is disabled and the actual background is restored.
