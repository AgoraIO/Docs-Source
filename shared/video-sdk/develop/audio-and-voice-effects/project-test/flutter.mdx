1. Press **Play audio effect**.

    You hear the audio file being played. Press the button again to pause or resume the audio. When the audio has finished playing, you see a `SnackBar` message.

1. Press **Apply voice effect**. 

    Put on headphones connected to your web browser app and speak into the microphone of your test device. You hear your voice through the headphones with the voice effect applied. The name of the effect is displayed on the button. Press the button again to test all the voice effects implemented in this example, one by one.

1. Tap the **Enable speakerphone** switch to turn it off. Speak into the microphone connected to your browser app. The audio is routed to the earpiece instead of the speakerphone on your test device. 

    Tap the switch again to turn the speakerphone on. When you speak into the browser app microphone, the audio is routed to the speakerphone on your test device.
