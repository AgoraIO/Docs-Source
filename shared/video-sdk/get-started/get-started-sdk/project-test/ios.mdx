Follow these steps to test your app:

1. Connect your iOS device to your computer.

1. Click Build to run your project and wait a few seconds for the App installation to complete.

1. Allows the app to access the device's microphone and camera permissions.

1. (Optional) If an untrusted developer prompt pops up on the device, first click Cancel to close the prompt, then open Settings > General > VPN and Device Management on the iOS device , and choose to trust the developer in the Developer APP .

1. Use a second iOS device, repeat the above steps, install the App on the device, open the App, join the channel, and observe the test results:
    * If both devices join the channel as hosts, they can see and hear each other.
    * If two devices join as the host and the audience respectively, the host can see himself in the local video window; the audience can see the host in the remote video window and hear the host's voice.
