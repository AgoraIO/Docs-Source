<Vg k="COMPANY" />'s <Vpd k="NAME" /> (RTC) SDK delivers ultra-low-latency, high-quality audio and video communication for one-to-one, group, or large-scale experiences. With cross-platform support and adaptive performance, it ensures smooth, real-time interactions even in challenging network conditions. Common applications include telehealth, education, team collaboration, gaming, customer support, live events, and more.

You can enhance the SDK with features such as recording, virtual backgrounds, and content moderation, or extend functionality through the Extensions Marketplace with AI-powered capabilities like noise cancellation, video effects, and more.

Agora’s Realtime Communication (RTC) SDK powers a wide range of real-time audio and video experiences. The following use cases highlight the most common ways developers use the SDK:

## Voice calling

Enable crystal-clear, low-latency voice communication across devices and platforms. Optimized for reliability even in low-bandwidth conditions, this use case is ideal for social audio, gaming, customer support, and other scenarios where scalable and secure audio is essential.

## Video calling

Deliver high-quality, real-time video for one-to-one or group calls. The SDK adapts dynamically to network conditions to ensure smooth video experiences. Popular applications include telehealth, education, team collaboration, and any app requiring face-to-face interaction.

## Interactive live streaming

Support real-time interaction between hosts and audiences with low-latency video and audio. Ideal for online classrooms, virtual events, or social platforms, this use case allows participants to engage directly with streamers for a more immersive experience.

## Broadcast streaming

Reach massive global audiences with reliable, high-quality live video. Designed for large-scale events such as concerts, sports, or live shows, broadcast streaming provides one-to-many delivery where millions of viewers can watch with minimal delay.
