<Vg k="AA" /> tracks and analyzes the usage, quality, and performance of real-time voice and video live streams and other <Vg k="COMPANY" /> products. Designed as a companion to <Vg k="COMPANY" />'s <Vg k="AUDIO" />, <Vg k="VIDEO" />, <Vg k="BS" />, <Vg k="ILS" />, and <Vg k="CHAT" /> it helps locate quality issues, identify root causes, and resolve problems to enhance the end-user experience. Monitor sessions in real-time with Call Inspector or use RESTful APIs to analyze usage trends and performance.

Extend <Vg k="AA" /> with the Datadog integration to bring Agora Analytics data into your Datadog account. Visualize and monitor metrics, set up custom alerts, and manage Agora data alongside other system metrics for a unified view of performance and quality.
