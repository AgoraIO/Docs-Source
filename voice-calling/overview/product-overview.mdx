Agora Voice Calling allows you to add crystal-clear audio chat to any application.  

Enabled by Agora’s Voice SDK, Voice Calling gives you full control over a rich set of features within your app. You can extend the SDK with functionality like recording and content moderation or use Extensions Marketplace to quickly activate noise canceling, voice effects, and more.
