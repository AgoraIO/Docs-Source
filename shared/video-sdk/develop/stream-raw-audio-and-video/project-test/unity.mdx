5. **Choose this sample in the reference app**

    From the main screen of the <Vpl k="CLIENT" />, choose **<Vpd k="NAME" />** from the dropdown and then select **Raw audio and video**.

1. **Join a Channel**:

    <ProductWrapper product="{['interactive-live-streaming', 'broadcast-streaming']}">
    1. Click **Join** to start a session.
    2. Select a role using the toggles.
        - When you join as a **Host**, the local video is published and played in the <Vpl k="CLIENT"/>.
        - When you join as **Audience**, the remote stream is subscribed and played.
    </ProductWrapper>

    <ProductWrapper product="video-calling, voice-calling">
    Click **Join** to start a call.
    </ProductWrapper>

1.  **Test processing of raw video data.**

    Press **Zoom In**. You see that the local video captured by your device camera is cropped and scaled to give a zoom-in effect. The processed video is displayed both locally and remotely. Pressing the button again stops processing of raw video data and restores the original video.

1.  **Test processing of raw audio data.**

    Click **Process Audio Frame**. Once clicked, your <Vpl k="CLIENT"/> begins processing and playing the captured audio frame, which is received through the `OnRecordAudioFrame` callback. Click the same button to stop processing the audio frame.
