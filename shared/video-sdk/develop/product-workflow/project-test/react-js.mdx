8. **Run the product workflow app**:

    1. In **Choose a sample code**, select **Screen share and volume control**.

1. **Join a channel**:

    <ProductWrapper product="interactive-live-streaming, broadcast-streaming">
    Click **Join** to start a session. When you select **Host**, the local video is published and played in the <Vpl k="CLIENT" />. When you select **Audience**, the remote stream is subscribed and played.
    </ProductWrapper>

    <ProductWrapper notAllowed="interactive-live-streaming">
    Press **Join** to connect to the same channel as your web demo.
    </ProductWrapper>


1. **Mute the local video**:

    Click **Mute Video**. You see your <Vpl k="CLIENT" /> disables the local video track. Press the same button again to enable the local video track.

1. **Test screen sharing**:

    1. Click **Start Sharing**. Your browser opens the **Choose what to share** window.
    1. In **Choose what to share**, select the screen to share. You see your screen in the local view of your browser and in the Web demo app. Press the the same button to stop screen sharing.

1. **Test volume controls**:

    1. Move the **Local Audio Level** range slider to the right side. Your <Vpl k="CLIENT" /> increases the volume of the local audio track and the remote users hear a louder sound. Move the slider to the left side to decrease the volume of the local audio track.
    
    1. Move the **Remote Audio Level** range slider to the right side. Your <Vpl k="CLIENT" /> increases the volume of the remote audio track. Move the slider to the left side to decrease the volume of the remote audio track. 

1. **Test media input switching**:

    Connect a new input audio or video device. Your <Vpl k="CLIENT" /> checks the device state and switches to the newly added active device. Remove the added device. Your <Vpl k="CLIENT" /> switches back to the previous device.
