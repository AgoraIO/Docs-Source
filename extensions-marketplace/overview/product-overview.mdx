Agora's Extensions Marketplace lets you quickly find and add functionality to Agora SDKs. You can easily add first- and third-party features like noise canceling and face/voice filters to <Vg k="AUDIO" />, <Vg k="VIDEO" />, <Vg k="BS" /> and <Vg k="ILS" /> with a click and a few lines of code. Extensions Marketplace makes integration easy, from discovering new features to activating and paying in a unified billing interface.
