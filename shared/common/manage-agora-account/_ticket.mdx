
## Submit a Ticket

This page introduces how to submit a ticket in Agora Console.

Follow these steps:

1. Log in to https://console.agora.io/[Agora Console].
2. Click *Support* in the top navigation menu.
  ![Image](https://web-cdn.agora.io/docs-files/1638341776363)
3. Type in your question or keywords to see if the question has been answered. If you cannot find your answer, select a category and submit a ticket to Agora's customer support. 
  ![image](https://web-cdn.agora.io/docs-files/1594950486528)

You can track the status of your ticket under *Recent Tickets*.
