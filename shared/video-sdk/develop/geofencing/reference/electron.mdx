### Geofencing area codes

The supported area codes are:

* `AreaCodeGlob`: (Default) Global
* `AreaCodeCn`: Mainland China
* `AreaCodeNa`: North America
* `AreaCodeEu`: Europe
* `AreaCodeAs`: Asia, excluding Mainland China
* `AreaCodeJp`: Japan
* `AreaCodeIn`: India

The area codes support bitwise operation.
