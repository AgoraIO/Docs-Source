Agora Flexible Classroom is a low-code solution that allows you to rapidly build scalable online classrooms of any size. With a modular design including the Agora Voice/Video SDK, Agora Signaling SDK, Interactive Whiteboard SDK, and Cloud Recording service, Flexible Classroom empowers developers to build online education apps more quickly with low code.
