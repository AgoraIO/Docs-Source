3. In Android Studio, open `app/java/com.example.<projectname>/MainActivity`, and update `appId`, `channelName` and `token` with the values for your temporary token.

5. In Android Studio, click **Run app**. A moment later you see the project installed on your device.

6. Select an option and click **Join** to start a session. 
    * When you join as a **Host**, the local video is published and played in the <Vpl k="CLIENT" />. 
    * When you join as **Audience**, the remote stream is subscribed and played.

    If your initial attempt to join a channel fails due to a restricted  network environment, the cloud proxy service is enabled and your second attempt to join a channel is successful.
