## Get started with Extensions

Extensions are add-ons designed to extend the functionality of the <Vg k="VSDK" />. The [Extensions Marketplace](https://www.agora.io/en/agora-extensions-marketplace/) is
home to extensions that make your app more fun. These extensions provide features such as:

1.  Audio effects and voice changing.

2.  Face filters and background removal.

3.  Live transcription and captioning.

This page shows you how to integrate and use an extension from the <Vg k="COMPANY" /> Extensions Marketplace.

## Understand the tech

To use a Web extension in your <Vg k="COMPANY" /> project, implement the following steps in your app:

1.  Add the extension to your app

2.  Enable the extension

3.  Use an extension feature
