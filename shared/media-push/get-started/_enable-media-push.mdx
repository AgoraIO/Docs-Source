<Vg k="MPUSH" /> enables you to stream audio and video from Agora channels to CDNs and other RTMP-based platforms. To enable <Vg k="MPUSH" /> in <Vg k="CONSOLE" />:

1. Log in to <Link to="{{Global.AGORA_CONSOLE_URL}}"><Vg k="CONSOLE" /></Link>, and click **Projects** in the left navigation panel.

1. Under **My Projects**, find the project for which you want to enable the <Vg k="MPUSH" /> service, and click the edit icon.

    ![](/images/media-push/project-list.png)

1. Under **All Features**, find <Vg k="MPUSH" />, and click **Enable Server Side RESTful API**.

    ![](/images/media-push/enable-media-push.png)

1. Read the pop-up prompt carefully, and then click **Confirm**. 

    <Admonition type="info">
    You cannot disable the service once you enable it. 
    </Admonition>

The <Vg k="MPUSH" /> panel shows an **Active** label. You have now enabled <Vg k="MPUSH" />. 

To process a media stream and push it to a CDN, you create a converter in the Agora channel. To create and manage converters, see [Use RESTful API](/media-push/develop/restful-api).
