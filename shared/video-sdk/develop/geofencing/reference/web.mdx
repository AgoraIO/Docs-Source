### Geofencing area codes

The supported area codes are:

* `GLOBAL` (Default)
* `CHINA`: Mainland China
* `NORTH_AMERICA`
* `EUROPE`
* `ASIA`: Asia, excluding Mainland China
* `JAPAN`
* `INDIA`
