### Geofencing area codes

The supported area codes are:

* `AgoraAreaCodeTypeGlobal`: Global (Default)
* `AgoraAreaCodeTypeCN`: Mainland China
* `AgoraAreaCodeTypeNA`: North America
* `AgoraAreaCodeTypeEUR`: Europe
* `AgoraAreaCodeTypeAS`: Asia, excluding Mainland China
* `AgoraAreaCodeTypeJP`: Japan
* `AgoraAreaCodeTypeIN`: India

The area codes support bitwise operation.
