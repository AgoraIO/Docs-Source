Digital rights management (DRM) restricts the playback or duplication of music to protect its copyright. Music files may only be played on specific devices or restricted by the number of playbacks. <Vg k="RTEE_DRM" /> enables you to find and play music from a DRM system.

## Understand the tech

To play music from <Vg k="RTEE_DRM" />, you implement the following steps:

1. Load the DRM extension framework. 
1. Create and configure an instance of the music content center.
1. Create a music player using the music content center.
1. Use the music content center to search music or download music charts.
1. Preload selected music on the music player.
1. Open and play music files.
