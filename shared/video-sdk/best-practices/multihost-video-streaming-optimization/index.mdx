In the scenario of multi-host streaming (4 or more hosts), audiences face the following challenges and needs:

    - Device performance and downstream bandwidth pressure: Subscribing to multiple video streams requires high device performance and audience bandwidth, especially on mid-to-low-end devices, which may experience lag and frame dropping, easily leading to a negative experience for viewers.
    - Flexible layout requirements: There is a need to provide a function for custom video layout, supporting viewers in adjusting the position of each host's video to meet individualized viewing needs.
    - Window switching and zooming needs: Among many hosts, the size of each host's video is limited. Flexible switching and adjusting window size can achieve a "thousand people, thousand faces" viewing experience.

Therefore, <Vg k="COMPANY" /> has launched a video experience optimization scheme for multi-host video scenarios, implemented through **client-customized composite layout**, centered on the viewer, to create a smooth and personalized video viewing experience.

This solution can be widely applied to the following scenarios:

    - Team battle PK
    - Multi-person conference
    - Online education for large classes
